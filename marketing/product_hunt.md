## FAQs

### 1. How is Levqor different from Zapier or Make?

**Short answer:** Levqor is API-first for developers. Zapier is UI-first for non-technical users.

**Details:**
- **Levqor**: RESTful API you call from your code. Built for AI agents and custom workflows.
- **Zapier**: No-code UI for connecting apps. Great for business users, limited for developers.
- **When to use Levqor**: You're writing code and need production infrastructure.
- **When to use Zapier**: You want visual workflows without coding.

### 2. What connectors are available?

**Currently supported:**
- Gmail (email automation)
- Notion (database and docs)
- Slack (team communication)
- Telegram (bot and messaging)

**Coming soon:**
- Google Calendar
- Airtable
- Discord
- Linear

**Request a connector:** Email support@levqor.ai with your use case.

### 3. How does pricing work?

**Simple usage-based:**
- **Starter (Free)**: 1,000 jobs/month, all connectors, community support
- **Professional ($49/mo)**: 10,000 jobs/month, priority support, advanced analytics
- **Enterprise (Custom)**: Unlimited jobs, dedicated support, custom SLA

**What's a job?** One API call to execute an automation task (send email, update Notion, post to Slack, etc.)

**No surprise bills:** Hard limits on free tier. Clear overage pricing on paid plans.

### 4. Is my data secure?

**Yes. Enterprise-grade security:**
- TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
- API key authentication with zero-downtime rotation
- Rate limiting (per-IP and global)
- Security headers (HSTS, CSP, COOP, COEP)
- Input validation and sanitization
- SOC 2 Type 1 certification in progress

**Data privacy:**
- Your data never trains our models
- Connector credentials encrypted at rest
- Automated backups with encryption
- GDPR and CCPA compliant

### 5. Can I self-host Levqor?

**Enterprise plan only:**
- On-premise deployment available
- Custom SLA agreements
- Dedicated support engineer
- White-label option

**For most users:**
Our hosted version is more reliable and requires zero maintenance. 99.99% uptime SLA, automated backups, and instant scaling.

**Interested in self-hosting?** Contact enterprise@levqor.ai
